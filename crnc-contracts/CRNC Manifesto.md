# CRNC Manifesto

CurrenC (CRNC) token is:

## 1. Sovereign by Design
- No administrators, no governance, no upgrade paths.
- No actor is granted authority to modify, intervene in, or reinterpret the system after deployment.

## 2. Supply-Final by Code
- The total supply of CRNC is defined once at deployment.
- The supply cannot be increased, reduced, minted, or burned.

## 3. Liquidity-Final by Action
- Initial liquidity is established through a single, irreversible external action.
- Liquidity provider tokens are permanently burned, making liquidity non-withdrawable.

## 4. Explicit, Not Adaptive
- All behavior is defined at inception.
- CRNC does not include mechanisms that adjust parameters, incentives, or behavior over time.

## 5. Protocol-Level, Not Social
- All properties are enforced by code and irreversible on-chain actions.
- No reliance is placed on coordination, discretion, or off-chain agreement.

## 6. Minimal by Intention
- No emissions, no treasury, no incentives, no roadmap.
- Nothing is reserved for future allocation.

## 7. Transparent by Default
- All state, balances, and actions are observable on-chain.
- There are no hidden controls, privileged paths, or opaque processes.

CRNC does not promise adoption, value, or utility.

It exists to demonstrate what a finished, constraint-driven token can look like  
when designed today.

What exists after launch is what exists forever.
